Chapel of Anticipation

  • Pick any class and keepsake you like. If you're unsure, Vagabond is a great beginner class and choosing 2x Stonesword Key allows you to open Imp Seals carelessly.
  • Start your playthrough and grab Tarnished's Wizened Finger from the chapel.
  • The Ring gesture is aqcuired if you pre-ordered the base game.
  • Similarly, the Ring of Miquella gesture is aqcuired if you pre-ordered the DLC.
  • Continue towards the boss and either beat or die to the Grafted Scion.
    • If you beat the boss, continue ahead and inspect the Nascent Butterfly at the edge of the cliff.
    • If you didn't beat the boss, don't worry we'll come back here later.

Stranded Graveyard

  • After respawning in the cave, talk to the Stranded Soul (optional) and take the plunge down to the Cave of Knowledge.
  • Rest at the grace and allocate your flask charges if you want.
  • There are no items in this cave, so once you are done, kill Soldier of Godrick for 400 runes.
  • Grab the Strength! gesture.
  • Drop down and activate the Stranded Graveyard site of grace.
  • Pick up Finger Severer and Tarnished's Furled Finger near the exit lift.
  • Take the lift up and enter the Lands Between!
