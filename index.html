Elden Ring Cheat Sheet

Comprehensive cheat sheets for Elden Ring, including the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC.

If you notice any mistakes, or want to contribute during the early phase, please open an issue on Github.

Credits

I'd like to thank ZKjellberg and smcnabb, the creators of the previous cheat sheets that inspired this project. The guide uses Fextralife and Eldenpedia for most of its information, along with Map Genie's fantastic Elden Ring Map to visualize item locations, ensuring everything is included. The beginning walkthrough sections loosely follow Cowboy's walkthrough for a head start to make your experience much more approachable. Thanks to Rin for designing the website's favicon and open graph image.
