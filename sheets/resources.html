Vigor: The stat you should focus on early. Getting 40 Vigor early makes the early-mid game much easier. Combined with the fact you can always start the game with a +5 Somber weapon and plenty of upgraded Flasks, you can be tanking and killing most enemies with little investment. It's not recommended to level Vigor past 60 due to diminishing returns.

Mind: Although the scaling drop-off on Mind happens quite late, most builds will be fine around 20-40 Mind depending on how many spells or weapon skills you utilize in your build.

Endurance: Level based on how much stamina and equip load your build needs. You typically want to end around 40-50 Endurance.

Strength: Strength scaling largely depends on your weapon and its individual scaling when infused with an Ash of War affinity. For an accurate scaling, use a Build Planner, add your weapon and play with the Strength stat until you see diminishing returns. Keep in mind that two-handing a weapon multiplies your Strength stat by 1.5x. For example, with 20 Strength, two-handing would raise it to 30, allowing you to wield weapons that require more Strength without actually meeting the requirements.

Dexterity: Same as with Strength. Dex scaling depends on your weapon and its individual scaling. Use a Build Planner, add your weapon of choice and play with the Dexterity stat until you see diminishing returns.

Intelligence: For the individual scaling on each staff, I recommend checking out Fextra's page about Intelligence.

Faith: For the individual scaling on each seal, I recommend checking out Fextra's page about Faith.

Arcane: If your weapon scales with Arcane, then leveling this stat will increase the amount of Blood Loss, Poison, Sleep, and Madness buildup your weapon inflicts. A list of all weapons that scale with Arcane can be found on Fextra's page. Arcane is also the only stat that increases your Item Discovery (1 per point, up to 99).

Tip: If you're unsure about which staves or seals are best for your build, try Jerp's Casting Tool Comparator

General recommendation:

  • Reach level requirements for your desired weapon
  • Focus exclusively on vigor. Leveling any of the damage stats yield little value, since most weapons have poor scaling at low level upgrades
  • Level Endurance to reach medium roll equip load with preferred equipment
  • Start focusing on your dedicated stat, once your weapon has better scaling
