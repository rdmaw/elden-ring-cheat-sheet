Vigor: The stat you should focus on early. Getting 40 Vigor early makes the early-mid game much easier. Combined with the fact you can always start the game with a +5 Somber weapon and plenty of upgraded Flasks, you can be tanking and killing most enemies with little investment. It's not recommended to level Vigor past 60 due to diminishing returns.

Mind: Most builds will be fine around 20-40 Mind depending on how many spells or weapon skills you use.

Endurance: Level based on how much stamina and equip load your armor and weapons need. You typically want to end around 40-50 Endurance.

Strength: Largely depends on your weapon and its scaling when infused with an Ash of War affinity. For accurate numbers, use a Build Planner, add your weapon and play with the stats until you see diminishing returns. Note that two-handing Hold Y/△ + L1/R1 or Hold E + Left/Right Click multiplies your Strength stat by 1,5, allowing you to use weapons that require more Strength than you have.

Dexterity: Largely depends on your weapon and its individual scaling. Use a Build Planner, add your weapon of choice and play with the Dexterity stat until you see diminishing returns.

Intelligence: For the individual scaling on each staff, I recommend checking out Fextra's page about Intelligence.

Faith: For the individual scaling on each seal, I recommend checking out Fextra's page about Faith.

Arcane: Level this stat to increase the damage of weapons scaling with Arcane. The amount of Blood Loss, Poison, Sleep, and Madness buildup your weapon inflicts will also increase. A list of weapons that scale with Arcane can be found on Fextra's page. Arcane is the only stat to increase your Discovery (1 per point, up to 99).

Note: If you're unsure about which staves or seals are best for your build, try Jerp's Casting Tool Comparator

General recommendation:

  • Reach level requirements for your desired weapon
  • Focus on vigor early. Leveling damage stats yield little value, since most weapons have bad scaling at low upgrade levels
  • Get enough endurance to reach medium roll equip load with your preferred loadout
  • Level damage stats when your weapon gets a scaling of B or higher
