West Limgrave (Level 10-20)



  • If you want, you can travel to Church of Vows and give Miriel the Royal House Scroll we picked up earlier. This will expand his inventory to sell:
    • Glintblade Phalanx: 2,500 runes
    • Carian Slicer: 1,500 runes
  • Travel to The First Step, head north and kill the Tree Sentinel for Golden Halberd and 3,200 runes.
    • Note: You could also return to this step later, if he's too tough.
  • Optionally head into the church and talk to Kalé for some slight dialogue about Blaidd.
  • Continue northwest past the church all the way until you reach the cliff wall. Look to your left and enter Stormfoot Catacombs.
    • Head down the stairs and activate the Stormfoot Catacombs site of grace.
    • Pick up Root Resin x2 from the room straight ahead.
    • Take the left path down and pick up Grave Glovewort [1] in front of the boss doors.
    • Head down the stairs and in the room below, pick up Grave Glovewort [1] from behind the pillar with no ghostflame.
    • Continue ahead and make your way to the first fire-breathing pillar straight ahead. Deactivate it by hitting the pillar once and pick up Prattling Pate "Hello" behind it.
    • Turn around and take the other path toward the second fire-breathing pillar. Ignore the ladder and hit the second pillar to deactivate it. Pick up Smoldering Butterfly x3, and Ghost Glovewort [1] from the room, but be wary of the five Imps that will ambush you here.
    • Head back and climb the ladder. Immediately to your left is a Grave Glovewort [1] you can pick up.
    • Continue into the room ahead and pick up Grave Glovewort [1] along with Wandering Noble Ashes in front of the coffin.
    • Turn back and immediately drop to the right. Pick up the final Grave Glovewort [1] and pull the lever.
    • Drop down and head toward the boss. Kill the Erdtree Burial Watchdog for Noble Sorcerer Ashes and 1,300 runes.
  • Exit the catacombs and follow the cliff edge south until you see a way down. Ride past the troll and head northwest toward an Invisible Scarab on the beach. Kill it to receive the Ash of War: Stamp (Sweep).
    • Note: A consistent way of killing them melee if you're struggling, is to line up on Torrent and start charging a heavy attack using R2 or Shift + Left Click as it comes toward you.
  • Continue northwest along the beach and pick up Strip of White Flesh next to a campfire and a Kaiden Sellsword.
  • Turn around and follow the shoreline south. Next to a tree trunk between two Land Octopuses is Land Octopus Ovary.
  • Continue heading southeast along the beach and notice the cave entrance to your left. Ride past it for now and speak to the Nomadic Merchant (West Limgrave) under the arching ruin. Buy any items you need from him:
    • Neutralizing Boluses: 600 runes each
    • Stanching Boluses: 600 runes each
    • Stimulating Boluses: 1,500 runes each
    • Smithing Stone [1]: 200 runes each
    • Armorer's Cookbook [2]: 600 runes
    • Broadsword: 1,800 runes
    • Club: 600 runes
    • Shortbow: 600 runes
    • Arrow: 20 runes each
    • Bolt: 40 runes each
    • Iron Roundshield: 900 runes
    • Note: Land Squirts: 200 runes
    • Note: Stonedigger Trolls: 400 runes
  • Continue east-southeast all the way to the end of the beach and next to the waterfall is a corpse with Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot.
  • From here, head back to the cave entrance you noticed earlier. Keep the mountain wall on your right side the entire time and enter Coastal Cave:
    • Head inside and activate the Coastal Cave site of grace.
    • Next to the grace you'll find Boc on the ground. Talk to him and he will warn you not to head inside.
    • Head inside and pick up Land Octopus Ovary just outside the boss room.
    • (Optional) Old Knight Istvan's summon sign can be found at the boss room entrance.
    • Kill the Demi-Human Chief duo for Tailoring Tools, Sewing Needle and 1,200 runes. (900 runes if you summoned Istvan.)
    • Continue through the boss room on the other side and pick up Smoldering Butterfly from the corpse in the long tunnel.
  • Exit the cave, head toward the church and activate the Church of Dragon Communion site of grace.
  • Head inside the Church of Dragon Communion and interact with the glowing altar. Buy any dragon communion incantations you need:
    • Dragonfire: 1 Dragon Heart
    • Dragonclaw: 1 Dragon Heart
    • Dragonmaw: 1 Dragon Heart
  • From the altar, look directly south and climb up the ruin for Exalted Flesh.
  • From the top of the ruin, head northwest for a corpse with Great Dragonfly Head x4.
  • Continue south along the cliff edge and on a platform below is a Somber Scarab you can kill that drops Somber Smithing Stone [1].
  • Continue directly east along the cliff edge for a corpse with Smithing Stone [2].
  • Travel back to Coastal Cave and talk to Boc next to the grace. Choose to "Give the Sewing Needle" to him.
