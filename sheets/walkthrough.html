Tutorial (Level 1-10)

First steps in Limgrave (Level 1-10)



  • Travel back to Farum Greatbridge and head directly west. Kill the lategame-scaled Somber Scarab behind the big stone pillar for Somber Smithing Stone [9].
  • Travel to Bestial Sanctum and starting from the golden tree next to the road, head east for an altar with Starlight Shards.
  • Drop down toward south and continue all the way down the hill, avoiding the poison traps on the way. Continue across the bridge (ignoring the boss here if it's nighttime) and activate the Lenne's Rise site of grace.
  • Use the Spiritspring behind the tower to get onto the cliff edge toward Dragonbarrow Cemetery with Golden Rune [1], Golden Rune [3], Golden Rune [6], and Golden Rune [8]. (4 pickups in total.)
  • Jump down again and this time, use the same Spiritspring to enter the rise from the rooftop balcony. Take the elevator inside to the top to find a chest with Memory Stone.
    • (Optional) You can always do the Lenne's Rise rune farm if you need some quick runes for an item or upgrade in the early game.
  • Travel to Third Church of Marika and head southeast along the beach. At the end of the beach shore is a Giant Crab feasting on a corpse with Strip of White Flesh x3.
  • From here, jump up into the woods and head southwest toward a round building. Enter the building from the other side, take the elevator down, and activate the Siofra River Well Depths site of grace at the very bottom.
  • Travel back to Third Church of Marika again and follow the clear path south into the woods. Continue until you reach a stone pillar next to a Runebear scratching a tree, then pick up Map: Limgrave, East from the pillar.
  • Pick up Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [4] below the bear and keep following the path southwest to find Thin Beast Bones x3 next to a wagon.
  • From here, head directly west toward two bats and pick up Golden Rune [1] from the base of the gibbet.
  • Follow the howling south toward Mistwood Ruins where you can find:
    • Golden Rune [2]. On a corpse west of the cellar entrance.
    • Smithing Stone [2]. Inside a chest in the middle of the ruins.
    • Axe Talisman. Inside a chest down the cellar next to a sleeping Runebear.
  • Exit the ruins and head northeast. You should see an Ash of War Scarab somewhere before you reach the Minor Erdtree. Kill it to get the Ash of War: Ground Slam.
  • East of the Scarab, at the base of the Minor Erdtree is a small stone container with Spiked Cracked Tear and Greenspill Crystal Tear.
  • Head south and drop down twice until you reach a Nomadic Merchant (East Limgrave). Buy anything you need from him:
    • Festering Bloody Finger: 1,000 runes each
    • Sliver of Meat: 500 runes each
    • Beast Liver: 500 runes each
    • Lump of Flesh: 800 runes each
    • Trina's Lily: 1,000 runes each
    • Smithing Stone [1]: 200 runes each
    • Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [5]: 1,500 runes
    • Armorer's Cookbook [3]: 2,000 runes
    • Hand Axe: 600 runes
    • Arrow: 20 runes each
    • St. Trina's Arrow: 160 runes each
    • Bolt: 40 runes each
    • Riveted Wooden Shield: 600 runes
    • Blue-Gold Kite Shield: 1,000 runes
  • From here, head southeast until you reach a fort. Head up the path with spiked barricades and pick up Golden Seed from the tree.
  • Head back down again through the spiked barricades and hug the left side until you reach the Fort Haight West site of grace.
  • Ride southwest toward the beach to find a corpse with Golden Rune [1] behind a Land Octopus.
  • Continue southwest toward Haight Cemetery for 4x Golden Rune [1], 2x Golden Rune [2], Golden Rune [3], and Golden Rune [4]. (8 pickups in total.)
  • From here, continue northwest, hugging the left wall until you reach a Giant Miranda Sprout. To the right, past the Miranda Sprout is a gravesite. Pick up Sacrificial Twig in front of the biggest gravestone.
  • From the Sacrificial Twig pickup, head directly northwest for a random junk item close to a Runebear. This item is probably just a randomized drop from common enemies, such as Glass Shard, Glowstone, Rainbow Stone or Golden Rune [1].
  • Travel to Agheel Lake North and pass time until morning at the grace, to avoid the night boss in this area. Follow the path southeast and pick up Arteria Leaf east of the Birdseye Telescope.
  • Northeast of the Arteria Leaf you just picked up, is an orange bush you can hit or roll through. Talk to Boc and exhaust his dialogue to get Mushroom x10.
  • Continue southeast back to the road and follow it until you reach an Ash of War Scarab. Kill it to get the Ash of War: Determination.
  • On the right side of the bridge is a pickup with Smithing Stone [1] x3.
  • Continue south across the bridge for a chair with Somber Smithing Stone [1].
  • Continue heading south and activate the Agheel Lake South site of grace just past the lake on a ledge.
  • From the grace, continue following the road southeast until you get to a long bridge. On the bridge is Smithing Stone [1] and a Stonesword Key further behind.
  • Activate the Bridge of Sacrifice site of grace past the bridge, on the right. Head back to the road and speak to Irina in front of a wagon. Exhaust her dialogue and choose Deliver her letter to get Irina's Letter.
  • Continue following the road and pick up Morning Star from the back of the damaged wagon.
  • Turn around and pick up Strip of White Flesh guarded by a Troll.
  • Pick up Golden Rune [2] and Smithing Stone [2] from some of the corpses further ahead.
  • Keep following the road, and in the middle of the road ahead is an Ash of War Scarab you can kill to receive the Ash of War: Mighty Shot.
  • Continue following the road and take a left before the big broken wall. Activate the Castle Morne Rampart site of grace.
  • Next to the grace is a Nomadic Merchant (Weeping Peninsula). Buy any items you need from him (but make sure to save at least 1,800 runes for a lantern later):
    • Kukri: 60 runes each
    • Smithing Stone [1]: 200 runes each
    • Smithing Stone [2]: 400 runes
    • Cracked Pot: 600 runes
    • Stonesword Key: 2,000 runes
    • Bastard Sword: 3,000 runes
    • Light Crossbow: 1,500 runes
    • Arrow: 20 runes each
    • Great Arrow: 300 runes each
    • Bolt: 40 runes each
    • Ballista Bolt: 300 runes each
    • Red Thorn Roundshield: 600 runes
    • Round Shield: 1,000 runes
    • Iron Helmet: 1,500 runes
    • Scale Armor: 2,400 runes
    • Iron Gauntlets: 1,500 runes
    • Leather Trousers: 1,500 runes
    • Crimson Amber Medallion: 1,500 runes
    • Note: Demi-human Mobs: 500 runes
  • Rest at the grace and pass time until morning to avoid the night boss here. Head south through the big broken wall and follow the road until you get to a stone pillar with Map: Weeping Peninsula. Be wary of the Archer Golem shooting at you while you're here.
  • From here, head west toward the golden tree on the ledge ahead to pick up a Golden Seed.
  • Head toward the damaged Archer Golem shooting at you and kill him (he doesn't respawn). Then, head east-southeast toward the hostile jellyfish on the beach. Pick up Somber Smithing Stone [2] and Arteria Leaf from the two separate corpses next to some chairs.
  • Turn northwest, hugging the cliff on your right side and head northeast toward a small poison swamp. On top of the second large rock further back is Golden Rune [4] at the base of a gibbet.
  • From here, head north to find a Teleporting Scarab you'll need to kill fast to get the Poison Mist incantation. If you can't kill it melee, I suggest using a throwing item like Kukri instead, which are conveniently sold by the previous Merchant for 60 runes each.
  • Continue north all the way up the hill toward the gate wall to reach a Misbegotten Warrior hacking on a corpse with Smithing Stone [1].
  • Either ride or travel back to Castle Morne Rampart, head west into the woods and pick up Yellow Ember in front of the house.
  • From here, ride northwest to the cliff-edge and jump down using the gravestones. Kill the Incantation Scarab at the bottom to obtain the Lightning Strike incantation.
  • Return to Castle Morne Rampart and head west back to the house. From there, head directly north and just left of the village is a second campfire with Golden Rune [2].
  • From here, head northwest and activate the Ailing Village Outskirts site of grace.
  • Head into the village and pick up Flame Crest Wooden Shield inside one of the huts.
  • Turn around and head into the church, south of the village. Beware the frenzied rats and pick up The Flame of Frenzy incantation and Sacred Tear from inside the church.
  • Travel back to Castle Morne Rampart once more and head northwest until you reach a pond with some Stray dogs. Turn east and at the base of the gibbet is Rainbow Stone x5, guarded by two Strays.
  • Turn around again, and behind the pond with the Stray dogs is a ledge with the South of the Lookout Tower site of grace.
  • Continue west up the path, cross the bridge and continue straight ahead until you reach the church at the top, where you can activate the Church of Pilgrimage site of grace.
    • (Optional) Rest at the grace inside for some dialogue with Melina.
  • Pick up the Sacred Tear in front of the Marika Statue.
  • West outside of the church is a small graveyard with Gilded Iron Shield and Blood Grease x2.
  • From here, ride south down the hill, keeping the mountain on your left side the entire time until you reach a small elevated surface. Activate the Tombsward site of grace on a ledge.
  • Ride west up the hill toward the church you can see in the distance. Enter the church and activate the Fourth Church of Marika site of grace inside.
  • Don't forget to pick up the Sacred Tear in front of the Marika Statue.
  • Ride back down the hill you just came from toward the Wandering Mausoleum, then head southwest toward a shack. Activate the Isolated Merchant's Shack site of grace.
  • Talk to the Isolated Merchant (Weeping Peninsula) and buy the Lantern from him for 1,800 runes. You can also buy anything else you need from him:
    • Lantern: 1,800 runes
    • Festering Bloody Finger: 1,000 runes each
    • Arteria Leaf: 1,000 runes each
    • Smithing Stone [2]: 400 runes each
    • Stonesword Key: 2,000 runes each
    • Lost Ashes of War: 3,000 runes
    • Zweihander: 3,500 runes️
    • Arrow: 20 runes each
    • Great Arrow: 300 runes each
    • Bolt: 40 runes each
    • Ballista Bolt: 300 runes each
    • Sacrificial Twig: 3,000 runes each
    • Note: Wandering Mausoleum: 600 runes
  • Don't forget to equip your lantern and from here, head northwest along the beach to find a Golden Rune [5] in the middle of the beach, and a Golden Rune [1] behind the fallen tree a little further back.
  • Continue riding northeast along the beach and pick up Golden Rune [2] past two Land Octopuses.
  • (Missable!) Travel to Church of Elleh, and now that you've heard the howling in Mistwood Ruins you can talk to Kalé to receive the Finger Snap gesture.
  • Travel to Forth Haight West and head northwest until you get to Mistwood Ruins. Look up with your camera to locate Blaidd and use the Finger Snap gesture below him to make him jump down. Exhaust his dialogue and he will ask you to find someone called Darriwil.
  • Next, travel to Agheel Lake South and head straight west toward a small campsite with Sliver of Meat.
  • From here, head down directly southwest, ignoring the enemies clashing, and picking up Crab Eggs from the edge of the cliff.
  • Head back up the hill northeast and hug the wall to your right. Turn right as soon as you can and from here there are two paths you can take. Take the path down first and pick up Ruin Fragment x3.
  • Retrace your steps and take the other path upwards. Pick up Large Club on a ledge surrounded by Demi-Humans at the end.
  • Travel back to Agheel Lake South and this time, head directly south and pick up Golden Rune [2] at the base of the gibbet.
  • Continue the path all the way up the hill and enter the Forlorn Hound Evergaol:
    • (Optional) Blaidd's summon sign can be found right in front of you after entering the Evergaol, if you followed his questline.
    • Kill Bloodhound Knight Darriwil for Bloodhound's Fang and 1,900 runes. (1,425 runes if you summoned Blaidd.)
  • Talk to Blaidd just after the fight to receive a Somber Smithing Stone [2]. (He gives it to you, regardless of whether you summoned him or not.)
