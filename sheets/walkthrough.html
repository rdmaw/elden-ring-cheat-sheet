Tutorial (Level 1-10)

First steps in Limgrave (Level 1-10)

Early Liurnia and Caelid (Level 1-10)



  • Sitting on the side of the road is Iji. Talk to him and buy anything you need: (If you're following the guide you won't need to buy any somber stones from him.)
    • Somber Smithing Stone [1]: 2,000 runes each
    • Somber Smithing Stone [2]: 3,000 runes each
    • Somber Smithing Stone [3]: 4,000 runes each
    • Somber Smithing Stone [4]: 6,000 runes each
  • If you tell Iji "Blaidd sent me" he will expand his inventory to sell:
    • Carian Filigreed Crest: 5,000 runes
  • Travel to Northern Liurnia Lake Shore, head directly east-southeast and pick up Somber Smithing Stone [3] from the corpse in a chair under the large bridge.
  • Continue heading directly south this time to activate the Gate Town North site of grace.
  • Head east-southeast from here, and once you pass the round stone building with the teleporter you used earlier, face directly east into the woods, and past the Giant Magic Miranda Sprout on your left is another round building with Smithing Stone [3] x3.
  • From here, head southeast until you reach a corpse on a chair with Smithing Stone [3] x3 surrounded by Miranda Sprouts.
  • Turn around and head northeast to activate the Eastern Liurnia Lake Shore site of grace.
  • From the grace, follow the path east, and make a right turn when the path splits. Follow the eastern path all the way down and enter the study hall. Activate the Study Hall Entrance site of grace for later.
  • Turn around again and back outside to your left is a large rock. On the leftmost side under the rock is a Somber Scarab you can kill for Somber Smithing Stone [3].
  • Travel to Eastern Liurnia Lake Shore and this time take the left path at the crossroads. Continue following the path all the way up to the very top while passing the green Flame Chariot on the way. Activate the Church of Vows site of grace outside the church at the top of the hill.
  • Head inside the church, talk to Miriel for some lore and buy any spells you need from him:
    • Magic Glintblade: 3,000 runes
    • Carian Greatsword: 10,000 runes
    • Blessing's Boon: 4,000 runes
  • Give the Academy Scroll to Miriel and he will expand his inventory to sell:
    • Swift Glintstone Shard: 600 runes
    • Great Glintstone Shard: 2,000 runes
  • Now that you've spoken to Miriel, be wary that a Bell Bearing Hunter can spawn here during nighttime.
  • In the back of the church is a chest with Gold Sewing Needle and Golden Tailoring Tools.
  • Head west behind the church and at the very edge of the cliff, facing the academy is a corpse with Stormhawk Feather x5.
  • From here, ride northeast past the church until you see a small gravesite on your left. Pick up Grave Violet in front of the big gravestone.
  • Travel to The First Step, and now that you've been to the Roundtable, VarrÃ© will have some new dialogue. Optionally talk to him and tell him "I'll fly straight and true!". Make sure to exhaust his dialogue.
  • Turn around and head behind the building in front of you. Drop down the leftmost part of the cliffside and just below on the stone pillar is Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot x2.
  • Jump down northeast toward some gibbets and near the hole next to some bats is a Smithing Stone [1].
  • Head northeast again toward Dragon-Burnt Ruins where you can find:
    • Twinblade. Inside a chest in a hidden cellar. Inside the building to the right, slightly separated from the rest of the ruins. You will need Torrent to get inside.
    • Crab Eggs. Located east behind the separated building.
    • 2x Golden Rune [2]. On two separate corpses in the middle of the ruins.
    • Stonesword Key. In a small building inside the ruins, next to a rat.
  • Head down the main cellar once you have all the items. Mind the rats and open the trapped chest to be transported to Caelid.
  • Open the chest in front of you for Gravity Stone Fan x6 and Gravity Stone Chunk x3.
  • Exit the tunnels by taking the path downwards. Continue down the ladder and activate the Sellia Crystal Tunnel site of grace.
  • Pick up Rot Grease in the corner of the room.
  • Leave the tunnels and head southwest toward the yellow glowing steps on the ground. Hit the Invisible Scarab as it's coming toward you to get the Poison Armament incantation.
  • Mount up on Torrent and ride east into the rot swamp. Pick up Rune Arc from the small island.
  • Ride southwest from here and activate the Inner Aeonia site of grace.
  • Ride northwest past the large Crimson Teardrop Scarab and continue through the ravine until you reach a dead end. Pick up Somber Smithing Stone [5] next to the School of Graven Mages.
  • Head back out the way you came and head west, hugging the wall to your right until you come across some Giant Miranda Sprouts. On a tree branch to the right is a corpse with Sacramental Bud.
  • Turn around and ride south-southeast toward Street of Sages Ruins where you can find:
    • Rock Sling. From the chest in a cellar. Inside the first building you would head toward from the Giant Miranda Sprouts.
    • Perfumer Traveler's Set and Perfume Bottle. In one pickup south-southeast from the first building with Rock Sling.
    • Meteorite Staff. On a corpse hanging through a window. In the final building southwest from the second building with the Perfumer Set.
    • Golden Rune [4]. On a corpse in a corner found as you exit the same room where you found the Meteorite Staff.
  • Continue southeast and hug the wall to your right side the entire time. Activate the Aeonia Swamp Shore site of grace. Rest here and pass time until morning to avoid a boss here.
  • Ride southeast into the rot swamp and on a small island is a Teleporting Scarab. Kill it fast before it disappears to get the Ash of War: Poisonous Mist. Alternatively you can use a ranged weapon/spell or throwing item.
  • Standing on the small island, look up directly southwest with your camera. On top of the enourmous white mushroom-covered rock are some enemies. Ride west-southwest and climb all the way up the rock. At the very top of the rock, surrounded by Servants of Rot is a corpse with Golden Rune [4].
  • Climb down southwest for a corpse with Golden Rune [3] at the bottom, behind the mushroom-covered rock.
  • Continue east toward a camp and a broken-down wagon you can destroy to find Crab Eggs x4 on a corpse underneath.
  • Head southeast toward the road and pick up Map: Caelid from the stone pillar.
  • Follow the path south of the pillar down and pick up Golden Seed from the golden tree next to the road.
  • Head back up again, and just east of the stone pillar is a Nomadic Merchant (Caelid South). Buy any items you need from him:
    • Cracked Pot: 1,500 runes
    • Stonesword Key: 4,000 runes
    • Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [15]: 4,000 runes
    • Arrow: 20 runes each
    • Bolt: 40 runes each
    • Flaming Bolt: 120 runes each
    • Champion Headband: 1,000 runes
    • Greathelm: 1,800 runes
    • Champion Pauldron: 1,500 runes
    • Champion Bracers: 1,000 runes
    • Champion Gaiters: 1,000 runes
    • Note: Gravity's Advantage: 800 runes
  • Head directly northeast of the merchant and activate the Southern Aeonia Swamp Bank site of grace.
  • Ride northwest toward the rot swamp for Smithing Stone [4] x3, Sacramental Bud, and Rune Arc on three corpses next to some chairs.
  • Head north-northeast into the rot swamp and pick up Aeonian Butterfly and Somber Smithing Stone [4] from the two corpses between the tree branches.
  • Continue north up the path and kill the Exploding Scarab ahead to receive the Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light.
  • Travel back to Roundtable Hold and since you've rested at a grace outside of Limgrave, Roderika will now be here. Exhaust her dialogue and she will tell you that she's still looking for her purpose.
  • Talk to Hewg about Roderika and he will mention that she has a gift for spirit tuning. Walk back to Roderika and "Tell her what the blacksmith said".
  • Go back and talk to Hewg again. Ask him "Would you watch over Roderika?" and "It's what she wants".
  • Rest at the table of lost grace (the big roundtable) and find Roderika in the same room as Hewg, who you can optionally talk to again for some additional dialogue. Talk to Roderika and she will now offer to upgrade your spirit ashes.
  • (Optional) You can also talk to Diallos now that you've met him in Liurnia. Exhaust his dialogue and he will reveal the location of Volcano Manor.
  • Congratulations! You've now completed the long and exhausting preparations part of the game! From here, I recommend using the Somber Smithing Stones [1-5] you got, to upgrade any Somber weapon you want to +5. If you're new and don't know which weapon to pick, I recommend upgrading the Bloodhound's Fang you got earlier to +5, which can easily handle any enemy in the early-mid game, though you will need 18 STR and 17 DEX to wield it effectively.
  • If you don't want to use a Somber weapon, that's fine. Just use your preferred weapon instead. If you have a weapon that you'd like to use for your playthrough, I'd still recommend using any Somber weapon +5 for now, while you work on upgrading your weapon of choice in the meantime.
  • I also can't stress enough the value of leveling Vigor early. If you're following the guide and using any Somber weapon +5, then you could even level Vigor all the way up to its first soft cap of 40, which will allow you to tank pretty much anything in the early-mid game, whilst still dealing good damage, making the game much easier.
