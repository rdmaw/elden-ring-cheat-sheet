  • (Missable!) Head inside through the windows and talk to Rogier. Choose "I'm here to defeat Godrick" and after speaking with him, immediately travel back to Rampart Tower. DO NOT rest at the grace as that will despawn him. Head back to the lift and Gostoc will be there. Talk to him and he will no longer stalk you through the castle.
    • Note: This spawn is inconsistent and I'm not sure why. If he is not spawning here for you, then I recommend running back and talking to Rogier again, which should be the trigger to make him spawn, then teleporting back to Rampart Tower without resting and he should appear behind the lift. This encounter is permanently missable if you interact with Rogier's bloodstain beneath the castle as that causes him to move, or if you progress their quests by killing Godrick.
    • After speaking to Gostoc here, he will stop stealing your runes on death and permanently relocate to his original position next to the main gate where he will now become a merchant. Buy any items you need from him:
      • Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot: 2,000 runes each
      • Ruin Fragment: 100 runes each
      • Furlcalling Finger Remedy: 1,000 runes each
      • Festering Bloody Finger: 1,000 runes each
      • Stormhawk Feather: 500 runes each
      • Caestus: 800 runes
      • Great Arrow: 300 runes each
      • Ballista Bolt: 300 runes each
      • Buckler: 1,500 runes
      • Bandit Garb: 1,500 runes
      • Bandit Manchettes: 1,000 runes
      • Bandit Boots: 1,000 runes
  • Make your way back to Rogier and buy any ashes you need from him:
    • Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble: 1,500 runes
    • Ash of War: Carian Greatsword: 2,500 runes
    • Ash of War: Spinning Weapon: 1,000 runes
  • Exit the church and continue until you get to a castle room with Golden Rune [2] x2 on a corpse in the middle.
  • Continue ahead until you reach a room with a Banished Knight. Open the chest inside containing a Mimic's Veil.
  • Head back outside through the northwestern entrance and to your left, climb the sacks and pick up Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot from the corpse on the stone balcony.
  • Head up the wooden stairs and where the wooden platform ends is a ladder on your left. Climb the ladder, run straight ahead and drop down onto the Exile Soldier below. Continue into the dark room to find a corpse with Stonesword Key along with a chest containing Pickled Turtle Neck x3.
  • Turn around and head back the same way. Open the shortcut and head down the stairs to the main room. On the ground floor, below the staircase is a corpse with Exalted Flesh.
  • Kill the Grafted Scion in the room (he won't respawn) and in the main room you can find:
    • Highland Axe. Below the large painting of Godfrey.
    • Stanching Boluses. On a corpse in the southern middle part of the room.
  • At the western end of the room is a lift that will take you back up to Rampart Tower. Rest at the grace if you need to and take the same lift back down.
  • From the lift, head into the first room on your right to pick up Crimson Hood, Golden Rune [1], and Lump of Flesh from three separate corpses.
    • If you haven't been following the guide and Roderika still hasn't moved to Roundtable Hold, then you will find the Chrysalid's Memento here instead. You'll then need to follow her questline at the roundtable until she becomes a spirit tuner. She will then throw away her Crimson Hood, which you can then return here to pick up.
  • (Requires: 1 Stonesword Key) Exit the room and head straight ahead for another room left of the lift. Unlock the Imp Seal and in the room you can find:
    • Hawk Crest Wooden Shield. On a corpse in the southern corner.
    • MisÃ©ricorde. On a corpse in the western corner.
    • Iron Whetblade. On the corpse in front of the fireplace.
  • Head back outside of the room and pick up Somber Smithing Stone [2] from the corpse below the statue.
  • Go back to the main room where you fought the Grafted Scion and this time, head through the northern room and immediately to your left for another room leading to a corpse on the wooden platforms with Arrow x10.
  • Drop down and head outside. Ignore the courtyard for now and immediately turn left outside for a cellar entrance. Inside the cellar is a corpse with Fireproof Dried Liver.
  • (Requires: 1 Stonesword Key) Unlock the Imp Seal in front of you and open the two chests for Godslayer's Seal and Godskin Prayerbook.
  • Head outside and on the corpse hanging over the edge is Golden Rune [4]. Make a note of this area, because you'll come back and drop down here later.
  • For now, turn around and to the left of the entrance is a ladder. Climb it and hidden behind a barrel is Arrow x10.
  • Continue ahead until you reach another ladder you can climb. At the top of the ladder is a corpse with Festering Bloody Finger x3.
  • Continue past the two Banished Knights and across the wooden bridge for another ladder you can climb. At the top is a wooden platform with Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [10].
  • From here, jump toward the top of the wooden guardtower and drop down the hole in front of you for a corpse with Wooden Greatshield.
  • Ignore all the enemies here and head directly northwest from the bottom of the stairs toward the big tree. Look north and pick up Mushroom x8 beside the stairs.
  • Head around and walk up the stairs in front of you for a corpse with Furlcalling Finger Remedy.
  • West from the top of the stairs, is a narrow passageway leading to a corpse with an item and a room with the next grace. Head into the room first and activate the Liftside Chamber site of grace.
  • Pick up Smithing Stone [1] x2 from the corpse in the narrow passageway just outside.
  • Head down the stairs, fight all the enemies here and in the courtyard you can find:
    • "Prophecy" Painting. In a room southwest of the fallen statue next to the Omen and the Stray.
    • Magic Grease. On a corpse in the courtyard.
    • Golden Rune [1]. On a corpse in the courtyard.
    • Golden Rune [2]. On a corpse in the courtyard.
    • Pike. On a corpse in the courtyard.
    • Smoldering Butterfly x5. On a corpse in the courtyard.
    • Golden Rune [2]. On a corpse near the courtyard entrance southeast of where you picked up the butterflies.
    • Golden Rune [4]. On a corpse in the courtyard entrance east of the Golden Rune [2].
  • From the corpse with the Golden Rune [4], head down east and kill the guards near the entrance for a corpse with Kukri x5.
  • Don't go any further from here, turn around and head directly west instead toward a second guardtower with Fire Arrow x12.
  • From here, drop down and head southeast back through the cellar. Walk all the way back toward the spot you noted earlier and instead of climbing up the ladder this time, drop down northeast, take out the ballista exiles and pick up Smithing Stone [2] in front of the wall near the three ballistas.
  • Walk down a set of stairs to find a corpse with Smithing Stone [1].
  • Continue down all the stairs and when you reach the bottom head southeast toward a door opening for Golden Rune [5] in front of a hole.
  • Turn the wheel to open the main gate if Gostoc didn't already do it for you, and activate the Gateside Chamber site of grace. If you rest at the grace you will need to take out the three ballista exiles up the stairs again.
  • From the grace, head east through the door for a corpse with Commoner's Simple Garb.
  • Head to the gate entrance and finally pick up Ballista Bolt x5, Golden Rune [2], and Golden Rune [1] from the three separate corpses.
  • Make your way up the main entrance and just before the three Exile Soldiers guarding the door ahead, turn east and kill the Lion Guardian in the corner for Somber Smithing Stone [1], Beast Blood, and Old Fang x2.
  • Head through the northeastern entrance and activate the Limgrave Tower Bridge site of grace for later.
  • Head back to the area where you fought the Lion Guardian and continue through the northern doorway. Jump up the rocks left of the stairs to the top, then jump quickly east toward the other cliff and onto the walled area to reach the Arbalest. You'll need to be quick, as the enemies operating the ballistas are no joke.
  • Travel to Liftside Chamber and kill the Omen at the bottom of the stairs to avoid dealing with him later. Head up the stairs east and past the first Warhawk, take the left stairs up toward a corpse with Smithing Stone [2].
  • Head back down the stairs and continue southeast toward a corpse with Golden Rune [2].
  • (Missable!) Look south toward a doorway to find Nepheli inside. Make sure you talk to her here, else you might miss out on a talisman later!
  • Pick up Smithing Stone [1] x4 from the corpse in the corner of the same room.
  • Head back outside and toward east for a Golden Seed at the base of the golden tree.
  • From the golden tree, head directly north toward an alleyway between the houses for a corpse with Smithing Stone [2].
  • Aggro the Troll on the stairs and make him follow you all the way back toward the fallen statue where you killed the Omen earlier. Bait the Troll into attacking the statue to get 5x Smithing Stone [1] and Smithing Stone [2] from the same pickup.
  • Head all the way back up the stairs where the Troll was originally located, and enter the room on the right. Activate the Secluded Cell site of grace in the room.
  • From here, head southwest through the other doorway and kill the Ash of War Scarab ahead to get the Ash of War: Stormcaller.
  • Kill the Living Jar Warrior in the back to receive Living Jar Shard and Raw Meat Dumpling the first time you kill it.
  • Behind the jars are 2x Cracked Pot from two separate pickups.
  • Through the southern doorway is an unnecessary lift leading back to Liftside Chamber. Take it if you want, but continue northeast from the cracked pots to find Kukri x8 on a wooden platform.
  • Jump onto the thin ledge northeast of the wooden platform. Follow the ledge and jump off toward the ledge with two doorways. Continue southeast for a corpse with Lump of Flesh x3 next to a Banished Knight and a Stormhawk.
  • Jump up the sacks to get back and head inside the doorway on your left. Once you reach the top of the stairs, turn around for a ledge you can jump to for Smithing Stone [2] x3.
  • Jump back and continue ahead to pick up Golden Rune [2] and Smithing Stone [1] from two separate corpses.
  • Head inside the doorway and at the bottom of the stairs inside is a corpse with Manor Towershield.
  • Go outside for a corpse with Smithing Stone [3].
  • Turn south and jump onto the thin ledge on the wall. Keep dropping down all the way and past the tower entrance is a corpse hanging off the wall you need to jump onto for Golden Rune [5].
  • Head inside and pick up Rainbow Stone x5. East in front of you is a shortcut door you can open leading back to Liftside Chamber.
  • From the corpse, look south and jump onto the broken rooftop platforms to get to a corpse with Arteria Leaf on the other side, guarded by a bat.
  • Head down the stairs and do a running jump onto the stone ledge leading to a corpse with Smithing Stone [2] x2.
  • Drop down onto the wooden platforms and pick up Golden Rune [2] at the bottom.
  • Continue ahead and take the right path behind the Giant Rat to find a corpse with Poisonbloom x3.
  • Continue around and kill the Glintstone Scarab to get the Rancorcall sorcery.
  • Drop down to the large arena below and kill the Ulcerated Tree Spirit here for Golden Seed.
  • Pick up Smoldering Butterfly x3, Stonesword Key, and Throwing Dagger x8 from the three separate corpses in the room.
  • Continue ahead and pick up Prince of Death's Pustule from the corpse.
  • (Optional) Turn around and interact with the bloodstain in front of the corpse for additional dialogue with Rogier and Fia later.
  • Travel to the Secluded Cell and prepare for a boss fight:
    • (Optional) Nepheli's summon sign can be found just outside the boss door if you spoke to her earlier. (Summoning her only affects dialogue, you'll still receive her talisman later as long as you spoke to her earlier.)
    • Head inside the boss room and kill Godrick the Grafted for Godrick's Great Rune, Remembrance of the Grafted, and 20,000 runes. (15,000 runes if you summoned Nepheli.)
  • Activate the Godrick the Grafted site of grace in the middle of the arena and talk to Gostoc who now permanently relocates here.
  • Head into the throne room and continue inside until you reach the bottom. Pick up the Shabriri Grape next to the ghost.
  • Head outside toward Lake-Facing Cliffs and talk to Hyetta just northwest of the grace. Choose "Give a Shabriri Grape" to receive the As You Wish gesture.
  • (Optional) Talk to Boc next to the grace and alter any garments with him free of charge if you want.
  • Travel to Limgrave Tower Bridge and head up the stairs for a corpse with Boltdrake Talisman.
  • Continue across the bridge and in the damaged crater section before the Archer Golem is a pickup with Warming Stone.
  • Climb back up and instead of taking the portal, drop down behind it to pick up Soft Cotton and Ash-of-War Scarab from two separate corpses.
  • Climb back up once more and use the portal to reach the other side of the bridge. Turn around and on the second balcony to your left is a corpse with Golden Rune [2].
  • Continue further and pick up Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot from the corpse guarded by two Stormhawks.
  • At the end of the bridge is a corpse with Stormhawk Feather x5 hanging over the right side edge.
  • Run all the way back to where you got teleported to, take the lift up and activate the Divine Tower of Limgrave site of grace.
  • Head all the way to the top and interact with the Great Rune. Choose "Restore the power of the Great Rune" to receive Godrick's Great Rune.
    • Note: Now that you've activated your first Great Rune, you can equip it at any site of grace and start using Rune Arcs to activate its effects. Similar to Embers from Dark Souls 3, the effect of Great Runes are lost on death, so you'll need to use a new Rune Arc every time you die to gain back the effects of your equipped Great Rune.
